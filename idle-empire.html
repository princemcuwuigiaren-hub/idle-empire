WORLD / CONCEPTS

World/Concepts is the future home of Vulcan, an industrial AI built to take ideas and test them against reality.

Vulcan will run sandbox and virtual reality simulations of your concept, generate improvement suggestions, produce 3D models where applicable, and deliver a full tested blueprint.

If your concept passes testing and you wish to build it, a formal legal contract will be issued, protecting both your rights and IDLE EMPIRE's intellectual stake in the innovation.

Vulcan is in development. Available in a future version of IDLE EMPIRE.

AI SYSTEMS

Two AI systems are planned for IDLE EMPIRE.

Morpheus generates goals from your ideas. It's running now on a Gemini API key you provide in Settings, as a stand-in until its dedicated model is ready. Use the AI option on any idea to have it draft goals when moving it to Projects.

Vulcan is an industrial AI that takes projects through simulation and reality testing. It will live inside World/Concepts, turning finished projects into tested blueprints. Still locked — future version.
